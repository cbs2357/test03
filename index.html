Definition Lists

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish defined by specially prepared, vinegared rice (shari or sumeshi) paired with various ingredients, most commonly raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, or egg. Contrary to popular belief, sushi does not mean "raw fish"—it means "sour rice"—and it is the vinegared rice, not the topping, that makes it sushi.
